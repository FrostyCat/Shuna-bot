Features

Everything your clan needs

Shuna automatically tracks player activity and delivers clear stats directly on your Discord server.

⚔️
CWL Statistics
Monthly per-player star breakdowns — how many 3★, 2★, 1★ attacks. Full history across past seasons with league info.
🏆
Family Clan Summary
Aggregated table of all linked accounts on your server, grouped by CWL league for easy comparison.
📊
Hit Rate Tracking
War Hit Rate (clean attacks only) and Legend Hit Rate — colour-coded indicators for every player.
🔄
Auto Backfill
Add a clan and the bot instantly fetches the last 3 months of CWL data. Refreshes every 30 minutes automatically.
🎫
Ticket System
Configurable ticket panels with categories, transcripts, and full management through the web dashboard.
🔗
Account Linking
Players link their CoC account to Discord — stats are automatically attributed to the right user.
